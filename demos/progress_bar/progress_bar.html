VSCP progress bar demo

This is just the standard HTML5 progress bar with some extra styling. It is connected to the vscp.ws.Client and configured to write the value to the progress bars from the CLASS1.CONTROL, Type=40/VSCP_TYPE_INFORMATION_LEVEL_CHANGED event.

You can update the slider by sending 

send 0,20,40,0,0,"-",90,1,20

Over the TCP/IP connection. The "90" is the value of the slider. For your convenience you can also open control_events.html in a new browser window and use demo 3 there to set the slider value to either 0,10,50 or 100.
